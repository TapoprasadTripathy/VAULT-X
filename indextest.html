Rewards Program

Progress Tracking

Bank Integration

Community Challenges

How VaultX Works

A simple three-step process to transform your savings habits

1

Create a savings goal

Define what you're saving for, set your target amount, and choose a lock-in period that works for you.

2

Deposit funds regularly

Make regular deposits towards your goal through our bank integration. Set up auto-deposits for convenience.

3

Achieve goals & earn rewards

Successfully complete your savings goal to unlock your funds and receive special rewards and cashbacks.
